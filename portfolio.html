1: "Debug by Daylight"

Info:

A horror game that me and my friends created. The game is about a python programmer student who is stuck on campus after school hours and is trying to survive until sunrise. The objective is to fix/debug Python codes from different computer laboratory rooms in order to escape this hellish nightmare.
